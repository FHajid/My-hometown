Things to do in Batam Island

Beach

Beaches in Batam offer a beautiful and diverse range of experiences, making them an enticing destination for travelers. Whether you're seeking relaxation, adventure, or entertainment, the beaches on Batam Island have something for everyone.

Maps

Did you know Batam Island is located very close to Singapore. It's a popular destination for both tourists and Singaporeans looking for a quick getaway. The ferry ride from Singapore to Batam typically takes around 30 minutes, making it a convenient and accessible option for those looking to explore the island's attractions, resorts, and leisure activities. This proximity has made Batam a popular destination for day trips and short vacations from Singapore.

Golf

Golf enthusiasts will find Batam to be a golfer's paradise, offering not only excellent golf courses but also affordable rounds that allow you to enjoy your favorite sport to your heart's content while taking in the island's scenic beauty and serene atmosphere.
